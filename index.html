About Darshan

Darshan Thoogudeepa is an Indian actor, producer, and distributor known for his blockbuster films and massive popularity in Kannada cinema.

Darshan was born in Mysore, Karnataka. He started his career from small television roles and later entered the Kannada film industry.


His breakthrough film Majestic (2002) made him a star. Since then, he delivered multiple blockbuster films like Krantiveera Sangolli Rayanna, Yajamana, Roberrt, and more.


Darshan is respected for his screen presence, action performance, and emotional roles. He is considered one of the biggest mass heroes in Sandalwood.
